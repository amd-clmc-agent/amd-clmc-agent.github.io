def src.nmpc_multicontact_python.mathematics.quaternion.Quaternion.toQuaternionMatrix (   self,
  is_conjugate 
) 
let us define 2 quaternions p and q, there multiplication is as follow:
\f{align}{
    p * q &= Q(p)
    \f{bmatrix}{
      q.w \\
      q.x \\
      q.y \\
      q.z
    \f}
  &= \f{bmatrix}{
    p_w & -p_vec \\
    p_vec &  p_w \, I_3 + skew(p.vec)
  \f}
  \f{bmatrix}{
      q.w \\
      q.x \\
      q.y \\
      q.z
  \f}
\f}
The conjugate correspond to the following:
\f{align}{
    p * q &= \hat{Q}(q)
    \f{bmatrix}{
      p.w \\
      p.x \\
      p.y \\
      p.z
    \f}
  &= \f{bmatrix}{
    q_w & -q_vec \\
    q_vec &  q_w \, I_3 - skew(q.vec)
  \f}
  \f{bmatrix}{
      p.w \\
      p.x \\
      p.y \\
      p.z
  \f}
\f}
:param is_conjugate: False represents \f$p*q = Q(p)q\f$
             True returns \f$p*q = \hat{Q}(p)\f$
:return: False returns $Q(self)$ /
 True returns $\hat{Q(self)}$
